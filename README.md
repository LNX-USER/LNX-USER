## About

I am a fullstack developer with 5 years of overall programming experience and 3 years of commercial development.

I build web services, game-related products, backend systems, dashboards, integrations, and deployment pipelines. One of the largest custom projects I built reached 300k users. I developed it solo, handled backend and frontend work, and maintained deployment and infrastructure.

My focus is practical engineering: clean APIs, reliable background jobs, predictable deployments, observability, and interfaces that are easy to use and maintain.
